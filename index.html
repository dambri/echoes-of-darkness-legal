⚔️ Echoes of Darkness

Your Destiny Companion on iOS

A powerful iOS companion app for Destiny players, featuring raid tracking, character informations, and comprehensive activity history powered by the Bungie.net API.

📱 iOS 🎮 Destiny Integration 🔐 OAuth Authentication ⚡ SwiftUI
Download on the App Store

✨ Features

🎯 Raid Tracking

Browse and track raid activities with detailed statistics, achievements, and completion history.

👤 Character Informations

View all your Destiny characters across platforms, including light levels, classes, and equipment.

🌐 Cross-Platform Support

Seamlessly manage characters across Xbox, PlayStation, and other platforms.

📊 Activity History

Comprehensive tracking of your game activities and progression over time.

🔒 Secure Authentication

OAuth integration with Bungie.net ensures secure access to your Destiny data.

🎨 Beautiful UI

Modern glass morphism design with custom Orbitron typography and gradient effects.

🛠 Technology Stack

Swift SwiftUI OAuthSwift Firebase Kingfisher Bungie.net API iOS 15+ Async/Await

Architecture Highlights

  • SwiftUI - Modern declarative UI framework
  • MVVM Pattern - Clean separation of concerns with ViewModels
  • Environment Objects - Centralized state management
  • Swift Concurrency - Async/await for network operations
  • Firebase Crashlytics Integration - Monitoring crash and errors

📱 Key Components

Authentication Flow

Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with Bungie.net, handling token management and refresh automatically.

Character View

Displays all Destiny characters with emblem backgrounds, class information, race details, and current light level. Supports platform icons for Xbox, PlayStation, and other platforms.

Raid Details

Comprehensive raid information including achievements, activity history, and detailed statistics for each raid encounter.

Dashboard

Central hub for accessing all app features with an intuitive tab-based navigation system.

🎨 Design Philosophy

Echoes of Darkness embraces a dark, space-themed aesthetic that matches the Destiny universe. The app features:

  • Custom Orbitron font for a futuristic feel
  • Glass morphism effects for modern UI elements
  • Nebula-inspired gradient backgrounds
  • Smooth animations and transitions
  • Native platform integration with SF Symbols

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Echoes of Darkness ("the App") is developed by Alexandre D'AMBRISI. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App accesses your Destiny game data through the Bungie.net API using OAuth 2.0 authentication. This includes your Destiny characters, raid completions, activity history, and related game statistics. We only access data that you explicitly authorize through the Bungie.net authentication flow.

How We Use Your Information

Your Destiny game data is used solely to display your characters, raid progress, and activity history within the App. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Bungie.net API — To retrieve your Destiny game data. Subject to Bungie's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — To collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostics to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Data Storage & Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device. We do not store your Bungie account credentials. Your game data is fetched in real time and is not stored on any external server.

Your Rights

You can revoke the App's access to your Bungie.net account at any time through your Bungie.net account settings. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Alexandre D'AMBRISI via the GitHub repository.
